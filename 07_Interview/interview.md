# Interview Layer

Guidance on how to communicate effectively during interviews for cloud and DevOps roles.

## Core Tips

- **Clarify Before Answering**: Repeat the question, ask clarifying questions to ensure you understand the scope.
- **Explain Assumptions**: State any assumptions you are making; this shows structured thinking.
- **Talk Through Trade‑offs**: Discuss pros and cons of different designs or solutions, considering security, cost, performance, and maintainability.
- **Show Ownership**: Describe how you would take responsibility for a solution, including monitoring, incident response, and continuous improvement.
- **Connect Security to Business Risk**: Translate technical risks into potential business impact (downtime, data breach, compliance penalties).
- **Use Simple Language**: Avoid jargon when possible; make complex concepts accessible.
- **Share Real Stories**: Prepare concise anecdotes that demonstrate your experience with the layers above.

## Study Tips

1. **Mock Interviews** – Practice with peers or use platforms like Pramp; focus on the communication checklist.
2. **STAR Method** – Structure answers with Situation, Task, Action, Result.
3. **Map Skills to Job Descriptions** – Align each layer you’ve studied with required responsibilities in the role you’re applying for.
4. **Feedback Loop** – Record yourself answering a question, review for clarity and conciseness.
